National Programs

Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated has upheld the principles of service and sisterhood since 1908. The 2022-2026 administration theme, "Soaring to Greater Heights of Service and Sisterhood," aims to continue this legacy by mobilizing active members to lead in change, education, and advocacy. The Omicron Chapter actively supports these national initiatives through each of our programming and community service efforts. Look for these logos on our flyers to know which specific initiatives we are targeting!

Strengthen Our Sisterhood

This initiative aims to deepen connections among members and reinforce the bonds that have been central to our sorority's impact for over a century. It includes various elements designed to foster intimate group connections, encourage cross-regional relationships, and promote spontaneous acts of care among sisters. It also features an annual day of united community service and a comprehensive leadership development program. The initiative seeks to enhance personal growth, amplify our collective impact, and ensure our sisterhood remains a strong foundation.

Empower Our Families

This initiative takes a multi-generational approach to strengthen families in our communities. This comprehensive program addresses key areas of family well-being, including childhood hunger, youth leadership development, mental health awareness, and senior support. The initiative provides meals for children in need, offers educational and leadership programs for youth, works to destigmatize mental health issues, and ensures active participation and support for senior members. The initiative seeks to create healthier, more resilient communities and continue our sorority's tradition of impactful service.

Build Our Economic Wealth

This initative seeks to empower individuals to implement key strategies for building personal, organizational, and community economic wealth. This includes the establishment of the first Black-owned, woman-led, sorority-based digital banking institution in the United States. It focuses on enhancing financial wellness, supporting women entrepreneurs, and leveraging our collective buying power to benefit Black-owned businesses. This initiative strives to foster a culture of economic empowerment within our communities.

Enhance Our Environment

This initiative takes a comprehensive approach to improve and protect our environment, focusing on tree planting, community and home gardens, waste reduction, and recycling efforts. It emphasizes the creation of community and home gardens to promote sustainable agriculture. Waste reduction efforts will engage communities in eco-friendly practices, and the annual Shredding and Electronics Recycling Day will safely dispose of unused electronics and paper documents. This initiative reflects our commitment to environmental stewardship.

Advocate for Social Justice

This initiative seeks to equip communities with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to activate their voices and mobilize self-help. It provides information about African American and other women of color in political leadership, as well as pathways for women interested in politics. The program also includes sponsoring public policy forums that discuss social justice and political engagement. Through voter education, registration, and mobilization efforts, we aim to empower communities to use their voices for change.

Uplift Our Local Community

This initiative inspires chapters to make exceptional impacts in their local communities. It also features a Chapter Collaboration Recognition Program, acknowledging chapters that have combined resources to maximize community impact. It offers local community service grants for notable projects completed by individual chapters or through collaboration. This initiative seeks to enhance the significant local contributions of our chapters and foster a sense of local identity in our communities.
