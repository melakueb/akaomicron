Signature Events

AKAreer Fair

Mid September

The AKAreer Fair is an annual event that aligns with our national program "Build Our Economic Wealth," aimed at empowering individuals through professional development. This event will feature interview and resume workshops led by experts and designed to help participants prepare effectively for the UC Career Fair. By participating in the AKAreer Fair, individuals can take significant steps toward building their economic wealth and securing meaningful employment opportunities.

Oratorical Tea Rose Speech Contest

Mid February

The Oratorical Tea Rose Speech Contest is a scholarship competition where participants present a 2-3 minute speech on topics related to the national program "Advocate for Social Justice." By engaging in this contest, participants not only hone their public speaking skills but also raise awareness about social justice matters. Through their speeches, contestants can inspire change and motivate others to join the movement for social justice, making a meaningful contribution to their communities.

Skee Week

Early April

Skee Week is the premier week of events, packed with fun, excitement, and impactful programs designed to engage and inspire! This themed week is dedicated to celebrating our initiatives through a variety of activities and service opportunities that foster community involvement and personal growth. We welcome you to join us as we come together to connect with one another and make a difference in our community.
