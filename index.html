Welcome Message

Dear Sorors and Friends Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, Omicron Chapter,

Greetings and welcome to the official website of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc.®, Omicron Chapter. It is with great honor, privilege, and pride that I serve as the 2024-2025 President. Celebrating 103 years, Omicron is ready to Soar to Greater Heights!

This year, I am committed to motivating the success of our members, excelling in all that we do, exemplifying sisterhood in each of our endeavors, celebrating our excellence in service, and embracing all of our wonderful gifts.

We are a unique, intelligent, and dedicated group of women who will continue to inspire sisterhood, scholarship, and service in the community. Together we uphold Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Incorporated’s mission of Service to All Mankind; we are committed to implementing the initiatives set forth by Alpha Kappa Alpha President and CEO, Danette Anthony Reed.

We will deepen our impact by fostering an environment of service, answering the call to action, and modeling inclusion and compassion for ourselves, each other, and the community. We are a chapter of top notch, talented, and educated women who are ready to serve.

We invite you to visit our site more often and explore how Omicron continues to stay active on and off campus.

Sincerely,
Imani Whipple
President, Omicron Chapter
